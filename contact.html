Before you get in touch

A few quick notes that may be helpful:

  • Most enquiries begin with a short call to understand your needs and priorities.
  • If you already have a deadline for a bid or tender, please mention it in your message.
  • You don’t need a fully formed project idea — we can help shape this with you.
  • We work with organisations of all sizes, including volunteer-run groups.

If you're not sure whether Oak Consultancy is the right fit, feel free to ask — we’re always honest about what’s realistic.
