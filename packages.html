Compare our packages

Choose the level of support that fits your organisation. If you’re not sure which option is right, we can talk it through and recommend a realistic fit for your size, workload and budget.

Basic

£50 / month

Best for smaller, volunteer-led groups

For groups who want to stay on top of realistic funding opportunities and dip into support occasionally, without committing to a large bank of hours.

  • Initial assessment of your organisation’s funding needs.
  • Light review of those needs twice per year.
  • Funding alert service for opportunities that match your priorities.
  • Option for Oak Consultancy to complete applications at an agreed hourly rate.

Recommended if your main priority is staying informed about what’s possible, with the flexibility to request hands-on help when needed.

Standard

£285 / month

Most popular

For organisations with regular funding activity

For organisations that want regular, proactive support with funding, planning and applications across the year – without everything falling on one member of staff or a few volunteers.

  • Up to 100 hours of consultancy support over 12 months.
  • Initial consultation plus quarterly review meetings.
  • Identification of suitable funding and regular alerts for new opportunities.
  • Writing and submission of applications on your behalf.
  • Support with monitoring and evaluation for funded projects.

A good fit if you expect to pursue several grants or tenders each year and want joined-up support across bids, planning and reporting.

Premium

£350 / month

For long-term, partner-level support

Our most comprehensive level of support, for organisations that want Oak Consultancy closely involved in funding, reporting and planning throughout the year.

  • Everything included in the Standard package.
  • 120 consultancy hours per year.
  • Monthly written update for your board or committee.
  • Attendance at your AGM to report on grants and fundraising performance.

Recommended if you are managing multiple projects, larger budgets or a higher volume of bids and reports, and want a consistent external partner alongside your team.

Hours can be spread flexibly across the year to match peaks in activity – such as deadlines, planning cycles and reporting periods.

What’s included with every package

Regardless of the level you choose, all packages are built around clear communication and transparent use of time.

  • Initial conversation at no cost or obligation before any work begins.
  • A simple written outline of agreed priorities for the year.
  • Tracked hours with periodic updates so you can see how support is being used.
  • Funding advice that’s realistic for your size, capacity and context.
  • Honest feedback on where bids or tenders are strong — and where they may be risky.
  • Clear next steps after key deadlines (successful or unsuccessful).

Which package is right for you?

The “right” package depends on how much internal capacity you have, how many projects you are running and how ambitious your funding targets are.

If none of these feels quite right, we can discuss a tailored arrangement based on a specific project, timeframe or combination of services.

  • Choose Basic if you mainly need to stay informed about opportunities and want the option of occasional, paid bid-writing support.
  • Choose Standard if you expect to pursue several grants or tenders each year and want ongoing support with planning, writing and reporting.
  • Choose Premium if you want Oak Consultancy to act as a long-term partner, supporting board reporting, planning and a higher volume of bids.

How pricing & billing works

We aim to keep pricing transparent and predictable, so you can plan with confidence.

  • All packages begin with an initial conversation at no cost or obligation.
  • Packages are usually agreed for 12 months to allow for planning and delivery.
  • Hours are tracked and reported so you can see how your support is being used.
  • Any additional work outside your package is agreed in advance at a clear hourly rate.

We are always happy to discuss budget constraints and explore what is realistic for your organisation.
