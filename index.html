Services

Whether you need to clarify direction, strengthen operations or support your team through change, Oak Consultancy provides clear, practical support tailored to your context.

Strategy Workshops

Facilitated sessions to clarify your organisation’s direction, priorities and measures of success. We help teams align around a shared picture of where you are now and where you want to go.

Ideal for: annual planning, new leadership, board away days.

Operational Review

A light-touch audit of your systems, processes and ways of working. We identify gaps, risks and opportunities, and provide focused recommendations you can realistically action.

Ideal for: growing teams, funding changes, process bottlenecks.

Change Support

Practical support to turn plans into real, measurable change. We can act as a thought partner, project support or critical friend as you implement new strategies or ways of working.

Ideal for: restructuring, programme redesign, new initiatives.

About Oak

Oak Consultancy works with purpose-driven organisations that want to make good decisions, not just quick ones. We bring a calm, structured and people-focused approach to strategy and operations, so your team has the clarity and confidence to move forward.

Our work is grounded in listening first: understanding your context, constraints and ambitions. From there, we translate complexity into clear choices and practical next steps that fit your capacity and culture.

We care about impact that lasts. That means building capability within your organisation, rather than creating dependency on external consultants, and leaving you with tools and insights you can continue to use.
