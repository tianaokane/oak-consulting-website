How working with Oak feels

We keep the process straightforward and as light on your time as possible.

  1. Initial conversation. We talk through your organisation, current work and what you’d like to fund or plan. You can also share any previous bids and feedback.
  2. Agree focus and priorities. Together we decide which bids, tenders or planning work to tackle first, based on deadlines, fit and capacity.
  3. Information gathering. We request key documents and details so we can write and plan accurately, without overwhelming your staff or volunteers.
  4. Drafting and shaping. Oak Consultancy prepares drafts, you review and add detail, and we refine until everything feels clear and realistic.
  5. Submission and follow-up. We support you to submit on time, and where there’s feedback, we use it to strengthen future bids and plans.
