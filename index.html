Our Services

We focus on three core areas where the right support can unlock funding and long-term impact.

Bid Writing

From smaller grants to larger, more complex bids, we help you identify suitable opportunities and prepare strong, clear applications tailored to funders’ requirements.

Available as a monthly subscription or one-off projects.

Strategic Planning

We work with you to develop realistic, achievable plans for your organisation or project, then align funding opportunities to help turn your plans into reality.

Ideal for boards, committees and growing organisations.

Tender Management

We monitor relevant tenders, highlight opportunities and support you to prepare and submit complete, compliant responses on time.

Helping you stay on top of e-tenders and local opportunities.

Our Packages

Choose the level of support that fits your organisation. All packages start with an initial conversation to understand your needs and context.

Basic

£50 / month

  • Initial assessment of your organisation’s funding needs
  • Needs reviewed twice per year
  • Funding alert service for suitable opportunities
  • Option for Oak Consultancy to complete applications at an agreed hourly rate

Standard

£285 / month

  • Up to 100 hours of consultancy support across 12 months
  • Initial consultation plus quarterly review meetings
  • Funding identification and alerts
  • Writing and submission of applications on your behalf
  • Support with monitoring and evaluation for funded projects

Premium

£350 / month

  • Everything in the Standard package
  • 120 consultancy hours per year
  • Monthly written update for your board or committee
  • Attendance at your AGM to report on grants and fundraising performance
