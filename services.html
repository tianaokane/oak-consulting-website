Practical funding and strategic support, tailored to your organisation.

Oak Consultancy focuses on three core areas: bid writing, strategic planning and tender management. We keep things straightforward, remove the jargon and give you the support you actually need to move projects forward.

Some organisations need a one-off piece of work. Others prefer ongoing, long-term support. However you choose to work with us, our aim is the same: to save you time, reduce stress and improve your chances of success.

How we work

Every organisation is different, so we start by understanding your context, capacity and goals. From there, we agree a realistic plan and level of support that fits your budget and time.

1. Understand your starting point

We begin with an initial conversation to understand your organisation, current projects, past funding experience and capacity to deliver.

2. Agree priorities and focus

Together we identify the most important projects or income streams to focus on first, and where Oak Consultancy can add the most value.

3. Provide practical, ongoing support

Support can be a one-off project or part of an ongoing package, with clear actions, timelines and check-ins so you always know what’s happening.

Bid Writing

From smaller grants to larger, more complex bids, we help you identify suitable opportunities and prepare strong, clear applications that funders can easily understand.

What’s included

  • Identification of relevant grants and funding opportunities for your organisation or project.
  • Review of eligibility, guidance and criteria so you only apply for realistic opportunities.
  • Drafting and editing of applications, including budgets and supporting documents.
  • Feedback on unsuccessful bids and recommendations to strengthen future applications.

Strategic Planning

We work with boards, committees and leadership teams to develop realistic, achievable plans that match your capacity and ambitions – and then align funding with those plans.

What’s included

  • Facilitated conversations with trustees, staff or volunteers to clarify priorities.
  • Support to define clear outcomes, milestones and measures of success.
  • Creation or refinement of strategic or project plans that funders can easily follow.
  • Mapping of funding opportunities to your plans, so bids are consistent and coherent.

Learn more about Strategic Planning →

Tender Management

We help you stay on top of e-tenders and local opportunities, from spotting relevant calls to preparing compliant, complete submissions on time.

What’s included

  • Monitoring of relevant tender portals and alerts for suitable opportunities.
  • Review of tender documents, deadlines and compliance requirements.
  • Support with drafting responses, gathering evidence and completing forms.
  • Project management of the submission process, including internal deadlines and checks.
