How we work with you

Every organisation is different, so we start by understanding your context, capacity and goals. From there, we agree a realistic plan and level of support that fits your time and budget.

  1. 1. Initial conversation. A short call to understand your organisation, current projects, deadlines and funding or planning needs.
  2. 2. Agree focus and level of support. Together we decide which bids, tenders or planning work to prioritise, and whether a one-off project or package makes most sense.
  3. 3. Information gathering. We request key documents and details so we can work accurately, without overwhelming your staff or volunteers.
  4. 4. Drafting and shaping. Oak Consultancy prepares drafts; you review and add detail; we refine until everything feels clear, realistic and funder-ready.
  5. 5. Submission and follow-up. We support you to submit on time and use any feedback to strengthen future bids, tenders and plans.

Our core services

You can work with us on a single area or combine services into a joined-up package of support.

Grants & funding bids

Bid Writing

Support to identify realistic opportunities and prepare clear, compelling applications that reflect your work on the ground.

Typical support includes:

  • Scanning for relevant grants and funding calls that fit your organisation or project.
  • Checking eligibility, guidance and criteria so you only apply for realistic opportunities.
  • Drafting and editing applications, including budgets and supporting documents.
  • Reviewing previous bids and feedback to strengthen future applications.

Ideal if you’re short on time, unsure how to “translate” your work for funders, or want to improve your success rate.

Boards & leadership

Strategic Planning

Facilitated planning support to help trustees, committees and leadership teams agree clear priorities and practical next steps.

Typical support includes:

  • Facilitated sessions with trustees, staff or volunteers to clarify priorities.
  • Support to define outcomes, milestones and measures of success.
  • Creating or refining strategic or project plans that funders can easily follow.
  • Mapping funding opportunities against your plans so bids are consistent and coherent.

Helpful if you have lots of ideas, but need a realistic plan that matches your capacity and can be shared with funders.

Contracts & e-tenders

Tender Management

Support to stay on top of portals, paperwork and deadlines so you can submit complete, compliant tenders with less stress.

Typical support includes:

  • Monitoring relevant tender portals and flagging suitable opportunities.
  • Reviewing documents, deadlines and compliance requirements.
  • Drafting responses, gathering evidence and completing submission forms.
  • Project-managing the process, including internal deadlines and checks.

Useful if tenders feel overwhelming or are often squeezed in around other work.

Not sure which service (or package) you need?

Many organisations start by talking through their current situation, then choose a mix of services that fits their priorities.

Good starting points

  • You have a specific deadline for a grant or tender and need help to get everything ready in time.
  • You know you need to improve your funding pipeline but aren’t sure where to start.
  • Your board or committee wants a clearer plan for the next 12–24 months.
  • You’d like steady support across the year, rather than rushing from one deadline to the next.

How we’ll guide you

  • We’ll ask a few simple questions about your organisation, capacity and deadlines.
  • We’ll outline the pros and cons of one-off work versus a support package.
  • We’ll always be honest about what feels realistic for your size, stage and budget.

You don’t have to come with a fully formed plan — part of our role is helping you shape it.

Ready to talk through your options?

A free 30-minute conversation is the best place to start. Tell us a little about your organisation and what you’re trying to achieve, and we’ll suggest a practical way forward.
